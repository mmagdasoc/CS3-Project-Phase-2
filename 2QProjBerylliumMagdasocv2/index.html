This website aims to educate people about the president’s incompetency and failures when it comes to governing the country and solving geopolitical and socioeconomic problems. The website is sorted in a way that makes it easier for Filipinos to open their eyes and accept the fact that he is not a suitable leader for this country. This is made in order to provide a safer future for the next generations by enlightening people to avoid voting for the right people.



This specific webpage would be an overview of his official ratings based on a number of factors. As we can see below, the president's satisfaction rating keeps on rising over time.


Pulse Asia Survey (COVID-19)




Nine out of 10 Filipinos approve of President Rodrigo Duterte’s performance amid the country’s fight with the coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) pandemic, a Pulse Asia survey revealed Monday. 91% of Filipinos approved the President’s performance, 5% disapproved while 5% were undecided.

Pulse Asia Survey (Sea Games)




President Rodrigo Duterte's approval and trust ratings surged in December 2019 , according to a Pulse Asia survey that was conducted during the country's hosting of the Southeast Asian Games. The latest public approval figure for the Philippines' chief executive is at 87% or 9 percentage points higher than the last month.

Pulse Asia Survey (Q4-2018)




In the SWS survey taken from December 16 to 19, 74% of Filipino adults said they were satisfied with Duterte's performance, while 15% were dissatisfied, for a net satisfaction rating of +60, correctly rounded. This translates to a "very good" rating, said Social Weathering Stations (SWS). The remaining 11% were undecided.
