Learnt Lessons and
Fruitful Futures

Even though Duterte's Presidency haven't ended yet, we cannot argue that it already left a sour taste on people's mouth. Let's put together all the pieces we have collected and analyze them for us to be well-aware next time especially since the next elections for the next President will happen in less than 2 years.




It is safe to assume that Duterte's incompetence outweighs the barely good deeds he did during his term.


More Research

In the age where online information can be readily accessed, there is no reason to not do research especially if they matters as important as this. The digital age however is a double-edged sword. Misinformation can be found everywhere from social media platform to rumours that you neighbor is saying. Do not conclude and do your own research first.

Spread Awareness

Many of the reasons why people vote for the wrong people is the fact that they vote based on popularity thus another win for political dynasties. Many people are also not informed or perhaps consuming media that has no factual basis. This is why we should educate as many as people as possible if we want the next elections to be fruitful. Educating one person is better than educating none. We should also encourage people to vote because one vote could turn the tides of the elections.

Hold Public Officials Accountable

The people need to stop with the "they are our leader, so we should always follow them." Our government is for the people, by the people, and of the people. They are called public servants for a reason, to serve the public and to uphold their needs. Once a person becomes a part of the government, their beliefs and values would affect how they manage people. If those people do something wrong, it is our right to complain and to demand more.
